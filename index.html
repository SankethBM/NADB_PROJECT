WHAT ARE WE !

Intellectual Property Protection.

NADB can assist businesses in registering and protecting their intellectual property rights, such as patents, trademarks, and copyrights. This service ensures that their innovations are safeguarded from duplication and counterfeiting.

Counterfeit Detection &Investigation

NADB can conduct investigations to identify counterfeit products in the market. This service helps businesses take legal action against those responsible for product duplication and counterfeiting.
